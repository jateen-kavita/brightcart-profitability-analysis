🛒 BrightCart Profitability & Marketing ROI Analysis

End-to-end business analytics project analyzing profitability drivers, product returns, and marketing performance for an omnichannel e-commerce retailer.
The project combines SQL analysis and Tableau dashboards to identify operational inefficiencies and recommend strategies to improve profitability.

📌 Business Problem
BrightCart sells products across eight categories through multiple digital channels:
🌐 Website
📱 Mobile App
🛍️ Marketplace platforms

📣 Social Commerce
Despite generating ₹1M+ in revenue, the company experienced declining net margins.
The CEO requested analysis to determine:
1.Which product categories are most profitable?
2.Which sales channels generate the highest profit?
3.How product returns impact margins?
4.Which marketing platforms deliver the best ROAS?
5.Where 20% of marketing spend can be reduced without harming revenue?

🧰 Tech Stack
🗄️ SQL (PostgreSQL) — data analysis and metric calculations
📊 Tableau — dashboard development and visualization
📁 CSV datasets — orders, products, and marketing performance data

🔍 Key Insights
📦 Category Profitability
Electronics and Toys deliver the highest margins (>30%), while Books and Beauty show significantly lower profitability due to shipping costs and discounting.
📱 Channel Economics
Mobile App transactions generate the highest profit per order, while Marketplace channels reduce margins due to platform commissions.
🔁 Returns Impact
Product returns represent a major margin leak, generating operational losses through refunds and logistics costs.
📈 Marketing Performance
TikTok Ads and Influencer campaigns deliver the strongest ROAS, while Email Marketing shows the lowest return efficiency.

🚀 Strategic Recommendations
📦 Prioritize high-margin categories such as Electronics and Toys
📱 Encourage purchases through the Mobile App to improve margins
🔁 Reduce return rates through improved product information
📊 Reallocate marketing budget toward high-ROAS platforms
⚠️ Reduce spend on consistently underperforming channels
